About

With the aim to beat the Covid-19 pandemic, this app has been featured with the zone detector, which tracks the current location of the user and notifies them of the zone they are present in. Thus, will make them aware and comply with the rules set for that respective zone. For the time, this app is under development and the zone tracker feature is only available for the state of Odisha.

  • Real-time Zone detection
  • Real-time Covid-19 Statistics of India and also the world.
  • Helpline numbers for the states of India.
